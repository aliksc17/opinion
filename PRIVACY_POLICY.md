# Privacy Policy for Opinion

**Last updated:** February 17, 2026

Thracian Soft ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Opinion mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information
When you register or sign in, we collect:
- Email address
- Display name
- Profile photo (if provided via Google Sign-In)
- Age
- Gender
- Country

### 1.2 User-Generated Content
- Questions you post (text and images)
- Votes and responses you submit

### 1.3 Automatically Collected Information
- Device type and operating system
- App usage data (interactions, timestamps)
- Anonymous analytics data

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the App's functionality
- Display questions and voting results
- Personalize content based on your preferences (country, gender filters)
- Improve the App experience
- Moderate content and enforce community guidelines
- Send important service-related notifications

## 3. Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored securely using **Google Firebase** (Firestore, Authentication)
- Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS)
- We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information
- We do not sell your personal data to third parties

## 4. Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services:

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---------|---------|----------------|
| Google Firebase | Authentication & Database | [Firebase Privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) |
| Google AdMob | Advertising | [Google Ads Privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |
| Google Sign-In | Authentication | [Google Privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |

These services may collect information independently. Please refer to their respective privacy policies.

## 5. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use cookies and device identifiers to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

## 7. Content Reporting

Users can report inappropriate content. Reported content is reviewed by our team and may be removed if it violates our guidelines. Reporter information is kept confidential.

## 8. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Update or correct your information
- Delete your account and associated data
- Opt out of personalized advertising

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

## 9. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be removed from our systems within 30 days.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the App and on this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
