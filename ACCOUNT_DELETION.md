# Account Deletion - Opinion App

## How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly from the Opinion app:

1. Open the **Opinion** app
2. Go to your **Profile** tab (bottom right)
3. Scroll down and tap **"Delete Account"**
4. Confirm the deletion in the dialog

## What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is **permanently removed**:

- Your user profile (name, email, age, gender, country, photo)
- All questions you have posted
- All your votes
- Your Firebase authentication account

## Important Notes

- **This action is irreversible.** Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.
- If you signed in recently, the deletion will proceed immediately. If not, you may be asked to sign out and sign back in for security verification.
- Premium subscriptions, if any, should be cancelled separately through the Google Play Store.

## Alternative: Request Deletion via Email

If you are unable to delete your account through the app, you can request account deletion by sending an email to:

**thraciansoft@gmail.com**

Please include:
- The email address associated with your account
- Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"

We will process your request within **7 business days**.
